About POP

POP:

In October 2018, Vijay Anand and Abishek Venkatesh decided to implement their entrepreneurial dream and make their vision a reality. Thus, Point One Promotions came into being.

The name of the company is a significant giveaway of the company’s mission and vision: to be the one-stop solution for anything online. Alongside that, Point One Promotions firmly believes in continuous and focused efforts towards a further dream. The success that comes is a very minor pixel of the bigger picture that is yet to be painted. This motivates the company to stay hungry and foolish and strive towards growth and development each day.

Furthermore, Point One Promotions accredits its success to your gut; know your gut and you’ll find glory, because the gut tells you the direction to move in. Adopting this approach has brought Point One Promotions 50+ satisfied customers with in the year of their inception. In this math with exponents, blooming opportunities and growth in the upcoming years is guaranteed.
